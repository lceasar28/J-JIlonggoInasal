J&J Ilonggo Inasal

J&J Ilonggo Inasal, located at Blk.72 Lt.28 Everlasting St. in Brgy. Rizal, Taguig City, commenced its operations on March 27, 2023, under the ownership of my older sister. Our business holds a special place as we prioritize ensuring the juiciness and freshness of our chicken. Though the plan to establish an Inasalan existed long ago, circumstances delayed its realization as I was engaged in other work. However, upon my cessation from previous employment, we ardently pursued this shared dream. What sets our food business apart is our commitment to serving exceptionally fresh and juicy dishes, aimed at satisfying customers and fostering their return. Offering superior quality chicken at a more affordable and budget-friendly rate distinguishes us from competitors. Our primary goal is to fulfill our patrons' needs and provide them with utmost satisfaction. J&J Ilonggo Inasal is honored and enthusiastic about serving you..
